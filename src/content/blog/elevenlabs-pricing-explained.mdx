ElevenLabs charges by character, not by minute. Here's the plain-English breakdown of every tier, what it actually buys you, and how to pick the right plan.

## How the Character Credit System Works

ElevenLabs charges per character of input text, not per minute of audio output. Every character in your script — letters, spaces, punctuation — counts toward your monthly allowance. A 1,000-character text costs 1,000 characters regardless of whether it generates 55 seconds or 90 seconds of audio (speaking pace affects duration, not character cost).

<EditorialNote type="warning" title="Credits don't roll over">
Credits reset at the start of each billing cycle. Unused characters don't roll over to the next month. If your workflow is sporadic — heavy one month, light the next — you're effectively subsidising ElevenLabs on your light months. Plan sizing should reflect your heaviest regular use, not your average.
</EditorialNote>

<InfoCallout type="tip" title="Regeneration counts too">
Regenerating audio costs the same characters as generating it the first time. If you generate a 1,500-character script and regenerate it three times before you're happy, you've spent 6,000 characters. On the free plan, this matters. On Creator and above, regeneration costs become negligible.
</InfoCallout>

## Free Plan: 10,000 Characters Per Month

The free plan gives you 10,000 characters per month with no credit card required. In practical terms: approximately 1,500–1,800 words of text, or roughly 10–12 minutes of finished audio at a natural speaking pace.

## Starter Plan: $5/Month for 30,000 Characters

The Starter plan triples the character allowance to 30,000 per month for $5. That translates to roughly 4,500–5,400 words, or 30–45 minutes of finished audio. It adds API access and expands the custom voice limit to 10.

This plan is the right choice for:

- Hobbyists and experimenters
- Freelancers generating a short sponsor read once a week
- Bloggers recording one article a month
- Developers testing integration possibilities

<EditorialNote type="note" title="No Projects on Starter">
The Starter plan does not include the Projects feature. This is a meaningful limitation for long-form content work. If you're producing content longer than 2–3 minutes, Projects makes a real difference to workflow efficiency.
</EditorialNote>

## Pro Plan: $99/Month for 500,000 Characters

The Pro plan provides 500,000 characters per month — roughly 75,000–90,000 words, or 8–12 hours of finished audio.

It includes:

- Professional Voice Cloning
- 160 custom voice slots
- Highest quality audio output setting
- Usage analytics

<InfoCallout type="tip" title="Quality upgrade at Pro">
The highest quality audio output setting available at Pro makes a noticeable difference on long-form narration — additional processing adds warmth and naturalness, particularly audible on audiobook chapters or hour-long course modules.
</InfoCallout>

This tier is appropriate for:

- Daily publishing creators
- Small agencies managing multiple client voices
- Developers with moderate API traffic
- Course library builders at scale

At $99/month, it makes most sense when AI voice generation is a core part of a revenue-generating workflow.

## Scale Plan: $330/Month for 2 Million Characters

The Scale plan is designed for businesses and development teams. 2 million characters per month is approximately 300,000 words — roughly 300 hours of audio. At this volume, the per-character cost is the lowest of any ElevenLabs plan.

Scale includes:

- Unlimited custom voices
- Professional Voice Cloning
- Dedicated support
- API rate limits that support production traffic

<EditorialNote type="info">
If you're evaluating the Scale plan, the per-character cost comparison with alternatives like PlayHT's unlimited pricing becomes worth running carefully. At very high volumes, the economics of different pricing models vary significantly.
</EditorialNote>
